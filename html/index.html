Hekka Token

Hekka™ Labs is a start-up targeting to become the largest blockchain driven chronic disease data ecosystem in South East Asia (Including India).

Hekka Intends to solve multiple bottlenecks in the region’s healthcare industry to benefit both medical practioner and patient data management, enabling an augmented individualized medical approach to combat chronic disease for 2 billion citizens.

Our Solutions

Hekka™ is proposing a two-pronged strategy to build the Hekka Ecosystem : Launch an ICO Token to fund and build the network, and then build a pluggable solution that enables participants to engage with the ecosystem without major disruption to their existing systems :

Hekka Token

Biomarkers from the vast number of South-East Asian patients can generate significant value through new decentralized approaches that move away from tradtional non-performing centralized systems.

Hekka™ Token:

Hekka Token

HEK will be an ERC-20 standard token complimented by an ATOM token (Points) system that will be responsible for all of the HEKKA platform’s primary sources of social-economic exchanges.

About Us

Hekka™ has set out to transform the digitization, storage and management of South East Asian healthcare data through the utilisation of DLT and blockchain technologies. Hekka will generate a significant network effect by integrating its founding partners extensive healthcare data networks and applications from South East Asia.

Hekka’s focus is to innovate and collect A1 classified chronic disease and Immunology data that is streamlined for AI. Enabling breakthroughs in disease diagnosis and drug development.

Hekka is led by blockchain, AI innovators, healthcare practitioners and experienced healthcare professionals from South East Asia and Europe. Our blockchain team has already built successful blockchain applications including Genesis for healthcare data auditing.

Our Partners

HEKKA is partnering with the following founding participants to accelerate and validate its ecosystem.

Curisin - Description

Curisin Corp is a US Corporation that owns a significant healthcare network platform in South East Asia (formally OurHealthMate) that includes 40,000 partnered doctors and 30 million patients in a countrywide network. The Company is launching a new platform in December 2021 to offer multiple diagnostic and testing services to millions of patients in South East Asia.

Immunidex - Description

Immunidex Ltd is an AI driven healthcare diagnostic Company that develops affordable tests and applications that can more accurately detect multiple chronic and immune derived disease. The Company is currently working on a more accurate blood test for COVID 19 using a panel of blood biomarkers and proprietary AI.
